# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and patient.
- Disagreement is fine; personal attacks are not.
- Harassment of any kind is unacceptable.
- The project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate to
  uphold these standards, up to and including bans from the project's
  communication channels and repository.

To report unacceptable behaviour, please contact the maintainer via the
email listed in `SECURITY.md`. All reports are treated confidentially.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at the link above.
